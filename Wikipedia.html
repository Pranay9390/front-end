    Early life

    Pawan Kalyan was born as Konidela Kalyan Kumar on 2 September 1971 in a Telugu family His father, Konidela Venkata Rao ,worked as an excise police constable , and his mother, Anjana Devi, served as a housewife. He has an elder brother Chiranjeevi, and an his elder brother Naga Babu. Kalyan was educated at the St. Joseph's High School in Nellore and later in Madras (now Chennai). he was awarded the title "Pawan" by the Isshin-ryū Karate Association after a public martial arts demonstration. In 1997, He also holds a black belt in Karate. Kalyan made his acting debut in 1996 with Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi directed by E. V. V. Satyanarayana. His second film Gokulamlo Seeta (1997), a drama film released the following year. Kalyan's third film was Suswagatham (1998) directed by Bhimaneni Srinivasa Rao and produced by R. B. Choudary. He co-authored a book titled Ism, which outlines the party's ideology.

    Career

    Political Career In 2014, Pawan Kalyan founded the JanaSena Party, marking his formal entry into politics Despite initial challenges, the party gained momentum, and in the 2024 elections, it secured all 21 assembly seats it contested. Pawan Kalyan currently serves as the Deputy Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh and represents the Pithapuram constituency as a Member of the Legislative Assembly (MLA). Pawan Kalyan began his acting journey with the 1996 Telugu film "Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi. " Over the years, he has established himself as a prominent figure in Telugu cinema, delivering notable performances in films such as "Tholi Prema" (1998), "Gabbar Singh" (2012), and "Vakeel Saab" (2021) His portrayal of diverse characters has garnered critical acclaim and a massive fan following. . Philanthropy and Personal Life Beyond his professional endeavors, Pawan Kalyan is recognized for his philanthropic activities, often supporting various social causes. He holds a black belt in karate and has incorporated his martial arts skills into his film roles. On the personal front, he has been married to Anna Lezhneva since 2013, and they have two children together. Pawan Kalyan's dynamic career spans multiple domains, reflecting his versatility and commitment to both his craft and public service.
